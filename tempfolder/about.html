About the FDA E-Library

The FDA E-Library Archives serve as a centralized digital repository for documents related to food and drug safety, policies, research, public announcements, and reports. Our goal is to ensure that vital regulatory information is accessible to the public, healthcare professionals, and stakeholders.

Our Mission

To promote and protect public health through transparent access to authoritative information on regulated products and safety standards.
